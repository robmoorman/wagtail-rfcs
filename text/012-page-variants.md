# RFC 12: Page variants

* RFC: 12
* Author: Rob Moorman
* Status: Draft
* Created: 2017-30-05
* Last Modified: 2017-30-05

## Abstract

Third-party apps that uses copies of pages to show different behavior (like
translations, a/b testing and personalisation) are lacking of user friendly
page maintenance. It's hard to find your origional pages besides the copies in
a single list or while clicking through the explorer.

This RFC describes a universal implementation how to support variants of pages
to use for third-party apps.
